Achievements & Awards

Our hard work on the field and in the community has been recognized with multiple awards across competitions.

DECODE Lower Mainland Qualifier · Jan 2026

Finalist Alliance

BotSquared and our alliance partners competed through the elimination rounds to reach the finals of the DECODE Lower Mainland Qualifier, earning the Finalist Alliance distinction against strong opposition.

DECODE Lower Mainland Qualifier · Jan 2026

1st Place Reach Award ×2

Recognized twice for outstanding community outreach, BotSquared's work with FTC News Canada, Girls 4 Tech partnerships, and peer team mentorship earned us the top Reach Award at the qualifier level.

BC Provincial Championships · 2026 Season

BC Championships Qualifier

BotSquared earned a spot at the BC Provincial Championships, advancing as the 3rd-ranked team from our qualifier. Competing at the provincial level against the best teams in British Columbia was a major milestone for our program, validating our engineering, outreach, and team development efforts across the season.
