Our Mission

BotSquared is dedicated to designing innovative, high-performance robots while fostering a culture of learning and collaboration. We believe in "Design with Intent, Execute at Scale." Beyond the competition field, we strive to make STEM education accessible and exciting for students everywhere, championing outreach initiatives that empower the next generation of engineers and problem-solvers.
