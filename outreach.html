Community Outreach

BotSquared outreach spans national FTC media, STEM education partnerships, student-led fundraising, and direct support for teams around the world.

Media & Representation

FTC News Canada

Representing Canada in FTC

BotSquared founded and runs FTC News Canada, a national platform representing the Canadian FTC community. With over 2,900 followers and 64,000+ views, FTC News Canada is one of the largest Canadian FTC media accounts, connecting teams across the country and bringing visibility to the Canadian robotics scene on an international level.

Through weekly posts, team spotlights, competition coverage, and educational content, we've built a community of 20,000+ monthly returners and helped bridge the gap between Canadian teams and the global FTC network.

STEM Education

BotSquared × Girls 4 Tech

Inspiring the Next Generation

In partnership with Girls 4 Tech, BotSquared participated in the STEM Buddies program — bringing hands-on STEM education directly to younger students. Our team members designed and led interactive workshops covering topics like cryptography, coding fundamentals, and engineering problem-solving.

By teaching concepts through fun, accessible activities, we aim to spark curiosity and break down barriers to STEM for students who might not otherwise have access to robotics or technology education.

Fundraising

Community Fundraisers

Student-Led Fundraising

BotSquared is a student-funded team. Beyond our school's annual $2,500 budget, every dollar for robot parts, tools, competition fees, and travel comes from our own fundraising efforts.

From Krispy Kreme donut sales to bake sales and community events, our team members take ownership of the full fundraising process — planning, marketing, logistics, and execution. These efforts teach real-world business skills while keeping our program running.

Community Support

Peer Team Support

Helping Teams Grow

We actively support and mentor other FTC teams, sharing design knowledge, coding strategies, outreach ideas, and match strategy. Teams we've worked with include AArcHive #4890, SlapSeals #28473, NOVATARD #24950, The Merge #33593, Syntax Terrors #34045, FoxSlide #29029, and Broboti #19176.

We believe the FTC community grows stronger when teams help each other, and we're committed to paying forward the mentorship we've received.
